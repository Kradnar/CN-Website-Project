Slytherin House

Information Page

Slytherin is the best of the four Houses at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry and was founded by The Great Salazar Slytherin.
When creating the house, Salazar advised the Sorting Hat to only pick students who had particular characteristics he valued most. Those characteristics included leadership, resourcefulness,ambition and cunning.

Many Slytherin students will often form close relationships, often acquiring leaders (in order to stand up to the lies of the other houses) which further exemplifies Slytherin's ambitious and cunning qualities. Examples of these included The Deatheaters, Draco Malfoy's friends and other such groups.
